DinePulse — Early-Stage Company
DinePulse

Restaurant operators are flying blind. Yelp reviews are weeks stale, competitors' reservation data is invisible, and social signals go unread. DinePulse ingests live review streams, waitlist demand, and social sentiment to give operators a real-time read on their competitive position — delivered as a weekly digest operators actually act on.

Live operational data
Live scraping pipeline
Pulls Yelp, Google, OpenTable, and Instagram data on a 24-hour cycle. Handles rate limits, deduplication, and schema normalization.
NLP sentiment layer
Fine-tuned classifier tags reviews by topic (food, service, wait time, value) and sentiment — not just stars. Operators see what's actually breaking.
Competitive benchmarking
Each operator gets a ranked view against 10–15 competitors in their micro-market, updated weekly. Built on PostgreSQL with a Next.js dashboard.
Weekly operator digest
Delivered every Monday. Three actionable signals, no noise. Pilot operators open rate: 100%. Two have already changed staffing schedules based on outputs.
