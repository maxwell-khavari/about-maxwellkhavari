DinePulse — Early-Stage Platform
DinePulse

Restaurants lose customers after the first visit. DinePulse turns order history and engagement signals into personalized SMS re-engagement, helping operators understand when and why customers return. We're building toward broader customer intelligence + retention infrastructure for independent and multi-location groups.

Data ingestion
Connects to restaurant systems and POS exports to unify order history, visit frequency, and customer-level behavior. Handles manual uploads during early deployments.
Retention signals
Analyzes recency, frequency, spend patterns, and engagement to flag at-risk customers and identify likely return windows.
Personalized SMS outreach
Uses AI to generate tailored messages based on what a customer ordered, when they last visited, and similar customer behavior. Not generic blasts.
Operator insights
Surfaces which dishes, days, and campaigns actually drive repeat visits. Early focus is on actionable weekly signals operators can test.

T2I bias overcorrection & reconstructive prompting

Text-to-image model developers are patching bias with demographic steering, but the corrections are overshooting. I'm building a prompt reconstruction system that calibrates outputs to real BLS/census distributions, not idealized targets. Evaluation pipeline is live; running batch comparisons across SD and DALL-E outputs now.
